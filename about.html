Who We Are

Our Story

Ecovive Farm Africa was founded with a passion for sustainable and ethical farming. Our journey began with a small herd and a big dream: to provide the highest quality livestock while nurturing the land we depend on. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted name in the industry, known for our commitment to animal welfare, genetic excellence, and customer satisfaction.

Located in the heart of Kenya, our farm leverages the rich, natural landscape to raise healthy, robust animals. We believe in a holistic approach to farming, combining traditional knowledge with modern technology to ensure the best outcomes for our livestock and our clients.

Our Mission

To be the leading provider of genetically superior livestock by employing sustainable and ethical farming practices, ensuring the prosperity of our clients and the well-being of our animals.

Our Vision

To create a future where high-quality livestock farming contributes to food security, economic growth, and environmental sustainability across the region.
