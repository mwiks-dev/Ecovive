Who We Are

Ecovive Farm is a pioneering livestock aggregation and value chain enterprise based in Kenya with a scalable model targeting East and Central Africa.

Our goal is to transform fragmented livestock supply chains by working directly with smallholder producers, regenerating underutilized lands for Sustainable fodder production, and delivering traceable, market-ready livestock to both local and international buyers.

Our Vision

To transform the informal livestock sector into a traceable, export-ready and inclusive value chain through farmer producers partnership approach. To ensure rural wealth creation and build sustainable agribusiness systems in ASAL regions.

Our Mission

To create resilient supply chains for quality livestock by empowering smallholder farmers, regenerating idle land for feed, and delivering traceable, export-ready animals to regional and global markets.
